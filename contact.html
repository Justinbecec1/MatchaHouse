Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you offer dairy-free options?

Yes! We have oat milk, almond milk, and coconut milk available for all our drinks at a small additional charge.

Can I book for private events?

Absolutely! We offer private event bookings for groups of 15 or more. Contact us for more details and availability.

Do you sell matcha powder?

Yes, we sell our premium ceremonial grade matcha powder in 30g and 100g tins. Ask at the counter or order online.

Is there parking available?

Street parking is available on Green Tea Lane. There's also a public parking garage one block away on Main Street.

Do you have WiFi?

Yes, complimentary WiFi is available for all customers. Ask our staff for the password when you order.

Are you hiring?

We're always looking for passionate team members! Send your resume to careers@matchahouse.com.
