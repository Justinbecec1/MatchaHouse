About Us

Our story, passion, and commitment to quality

Our Story

Matcha House was born from a simple dream: to share the authentic taste and wellness benefits of premium Japanese matcha with our community. Founded in 2020, our journey began when our founder, after spending years in Kyoto learning the art of tea preparation, decided to bring this cherished tradition home.

What started as a small corner cafe has grown into a beloved destination for matcha enthusiasts and curious newcomers alike. We've stayed true to our roots, maintaining the same commitment to quality and authenticity that inspired us from the very beginning.

Today, Matcha House stands as a bridge between ancient Japanese tea culture and modern cafe culture, offering a unique experience that honors tradition while embracing innovation.

Our founder, Sarah Chen, spent three transformative years in Kyoto's historic Uji region, apprenticing under master tea artisans who have perfected their craft over generations. She learned not just the techniques, but the philosophy behind the perfect cup of matcha – the importance of mindfulness, respect for ingredients, and the art of creating moments of tranquility in our busy lives.

When Sarah returned home, she knew she had to share this experience. She envisioned a space where the ancient art of Japanese tea ceremony could meet the warmth of a neighborhood cafe. A place where busy professionals could find a moment of zen, where students could discover a healthier alternative to coffee, and where tea lovers could experience the real thing.

Our Philosophy

The principles that guide our craft

Ichigo Ichie - One Time, One Meeting

This ancient Japanese concept reminds us that every encounter is unique and should be treasured. At Matcha House, we embrace this philosophy in everything we do. Each cup we prepare is an opportunity to create a meaningful moment for our guests.

We believe that the simple act of enjoying a cup of matcha can be transformative. In our fast-paced world, taking a moment to appreciate the vibrant green color, the earthy aroma, and the smooth umami flavor is a form of mindfulness that can bring clarity and calm to your day.

Our team is trained not just in the technical aspects of tea preparation, but in the art of hospitality. We want every visit to Matcha House to feel like a mini-retreat from the stresses of daily life. Whether you're grabbing a quick matcha latte on your way to work or settling in for a leisurely afternoon with a ceremonial bowl, we're honored to be part of your day.

We also believe in continuous learning and improvement. Our team regularly participates in workshops and tastings, and we stay connected with our suppliers in Japan to learn about new harvests and techniques. This commitment to growth ensures that we're always offering you the best possible experience.

Our Process

From leaf to cup, excellence at every step

Sourcing

We partner directly with tea farmers in Uji, Kyoto, ensuring we receive only the finest first-harvest leaves shade-grown for optimal flavor and nutrition.

Stone Grinding

Our matcha is traditionally stone-ground to preserve its vibrant color, delicate flavor, and maximum nutritional benefits.

Preparation

Each drink is prepared fresh using traditional bamboo whisks and precise water temperatures for the perfect cup every time.
